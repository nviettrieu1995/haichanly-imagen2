# Heaven Father Nalas DNA

This is the locked visual DNA for Cha Nalas Nalanda in true heaven, thien duong, thien gioi, tuelinh homeland, celestial temple, paradise, dream/cosmic memory, and other explicitly celestial scenes.

Canonical reference image:
`nalas_chapters_08_86/character_refs/heaven_father_canonical/heaven_father_canonical.png`

Attach this reference only to true heaven/celestial lanes where divine Father Nalas is visible. Do not attach it to ordinary Earth/pham-tran teacher scenes.

## Stable Identity

- Visual register: Western sacred / traditional Christian Chua / Sacred-Heart-Jesus, close enough that the audience immediately reads a familiar Jesus-like sacred Father rather than a generic fantasy deity.
- Age read: one fixed traditional Jesus-like portrait in every lane, apparent age 40-42. Fatherly rather than boyish or elderly. Not 20s, not youthful 30s, not teenage, not baby-faced, not a handsome modern actor, not fashion-model youthful, not 45+ old Father, not 50s/60s, not elderly, and not allowed to drift younger/older from lane to lane.
- Face: compassionate mature Mediterranean/Semitic-looking face, warm olive/light-tan skin, deep calm eyes, gentle strength, slight mature smile lines and calm gravity without grey/white age markers.
- Hair: center-parted shoulder-length wavy dark chestnut-brown hair, classic Jesus silhouette.
- Beard: full neat brown beard and moustache. No long white beard.
- Clothing: luminous ivory-white robe with subtle gold trim, simple and sacred.
- Light: sacred-heart style radiant heart or gentle inner light, white-gold surrounding glow, open-handed teaching authority, soft sacred head glow acceptable in heaven.
- Setting: cathedral-like white-gold heaven, Renaissance/Baroque sacred calm, marble/ivory/gold atmosphere, clouds/light, tuelinhs/angels/light messengers when supported by the excerpt.

## Must Not Drift

- Do not make him the short-haired Vietnamese pham-tran teacher in a shirt.
- Do not make him a young clean-shaven messenger or a slim student.
- Do not make him a handsome young fantasy savior, youthful 30s actor-Jesus, modern-actor Jesus, or fashion-model version of Jesus.
- Do not make him shift between a youthful 30s Jesus actor, a 45+ older Father, and an old grandfather God-Father.
- Do not make him an elderly God-Father with grey/white hair or a long white beard.
- Do not make a younger version in one lane and an older version in another lane; preserve the canonical reference face and age every time.
- Do not make him visually identical to Giac, Chap, or any of the five messengers.
- Do not change his age, hair color, beard style, or face type from lane to lane.
- When he shares a frame with Giac, Chap, tuelinhs, or other messengers, Father Nalas must remain the unique sacred Father anchor: closest to the canonical reference, warmest white-gold heart light, most authoritative presence. Messengers must not copy his exact face, full Jesus-like beard, radiant-heart identity, chest-heart light, or robe silhouette.
- Only Father Nalas may carry the complete signature of center-parted shoulder-length chestnut hair, full neat brown beard and moustache, ivory-gold robe, and radiant heart. Other male tuelinhs, attendants, or messengers must have different faces, lower light intensity, shorter or tied-back hair, clean-shaven or light-stubble faces, distinct robe accents, and absolutely no Sacred Heart, glowing heart icon, heart-shaped chest light, or radiant chest emblem. If one attendant has longer hair, he must not also have a full beard, ivory-gold Father robe, or any chest-heart radiance.
- Do not use the pham-tran reference image for pure heaven lanes; use it only if the sleeping or waking Earth body is explicitly visible as a separate layer.

## Relationship To Giac And Chap

- Father Nalas is central, warmer, more authoritative, and more compassionate than the messengers.
- Giac remains leaner, restrained gold-white, analytical, with geometry/thread marker.
- Chap remains softer, rose-gold, protective, with heart/sash marker.
- Father must not borrow Giac's precise lean look or Chap's youthful rose-gold messenger look.
