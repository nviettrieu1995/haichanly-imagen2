# Five Messengers DNA

This is the locked visual DNA for the five messengers of the first tuelinh. Use it whenever a lane mentions Giac, Chap, two messengers, several messengers, or the full five-member group.

## Shared Group Identity

- Western sacred / celestial visual grammar in heaven: ivory-white garments, quiet white-gold space, mature calm faces, no Chinese/xianxia styling.
- All five true messengers are mature men. They are a coordinated working group, not generic identical angels, Father Nalas clones, students, monks, women, children, or the same white-haired man repeated.
- Use distinct face type, aura color, posture, hand gesture, and role marker for each messenger.
- If Father Nalas appears in the same frame, only Father Nalas may have a Sacred Heart, heart-shaped chest light, glowing heart icon, or radiant chest emblem. The messengers use role-colored sashes, hand light, geometry, grids, healing particles, or transit arcs instead.
- No ornate crowns, halos, Buddhist monk costume, church props in Earth scenes, horror-demon staging, or readable text.

## 1. Giac

- Role: insight, discernment, clear seeing, testing intellectual arrogance.
- Face/body: slightly older than Chap, leaner oval face, higher cheekbones, calm analytical eyes.
- Color: restrained gold-white.
- Posture: precise, upright, almost still, observant.
- Marker: small gold-white geometry or clear-light thread near hands/chest.
- Position: often nearest Father when asking, measuring, or observing.
- Do not: give him Chap's rose-gold warmth, a heavy Jesus-like beard, halo, crown, or generic angel sameness.

## 2. Chap

- Role: compassion, attachment-testing, protective response to suffering.
- Face/body: slightly younger or softer than Giac, fuller kinder face, warmer eyes.
- Color: rose-gold or amber-white.
- Posture: humble, protective, emotionally responsive but not weak.
- Marker: rose-gold sash, warm rose light held in the palm, or open palm near the heart or lowered toward suffering people.
- Do not: clone Giac's lean analytical face, gold-white geometry marker, cold judge posture, or the Father's Sacred Heart / glowing chest emblem.

## 3. Messenger Three

- Role: order, law, causal record, operating structure.
- Color: blue-white.
- Posture: upright, symmetrical, composed.
- Marker: luminous blue-white grid, archive table, or orderly light tablets with no readable text.
- Do not: make this figure comforting like Chap or golden like Giac.

## 4. Messenger Four

- Role: healing, transformation, toxin removal, activation of goodness.
- Color: green-gold.
- Posture: gentle guardian, grounded, restorative.
- Marker: green-gold healing current, clean botanical light, or particles clearing black toxins.
- Do not: make this figure demonic, horror-like, plague-like, or frightening.

## 5. Messenger Five

- Role: transmission, transit, path between heaven, Earth, and the transiting planet.
- Color: silver-violet.
- Posture: slender, quiet guide, body slightly turned as if opening a route.
- Marker: silver-violet gateway, path, transit arc, or ribbon between realms.
- Do not: make this figure another gold-white Giac or another rose-gold Chap.

## Pair Rule: Giac + Chap

When Giac and Chap appear together, the frame must make their roles visible at a glance:

- Giac: gold-white, lean, precise, analytical, geometry/thread marker.
- Chap: rose-gold, softer, compassionate, protective, sash or palm-light marker.

Never give them the same face, same hair, same beard, same robe silhouette, same aura color, or the divine Father / Jesus-like look.

## Chapter 8 Messenger Flow Lock

- In the early Chapter 8 dream, the dragon transforms into two adult men. These are male messengers in human form. Do not show a man/woman pair, two girls, children, or romantic couple composition.
- When Giac and Chap watch or guide Nalas before the disguise sequence, they remain two mature male messengers with distinct role markers.
- A messenger may appear as an old man only when the local excerpt says that disguise appears.
- A messenger may appear as an old woman only when the local excerpt says that disguise appears. This is a temporary disguise, not the messenger's true identity.
- The old man and old woman appear together only after Nalas has met the woman teacher. Do not use that old-man/old-woman pair for the earlier dragon-transforms-into-two-men beat.
